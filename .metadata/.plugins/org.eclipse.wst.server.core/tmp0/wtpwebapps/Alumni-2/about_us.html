UCT Alumni Network

About Us

Who

UCT Computer Science Alumni network is a professional space where former students and employees of the
Computer Science department interact and stay connected with each other. The network also includes the
department's potential graduates who aim to establish connections former students and professionals in the industry.

What

The UCT department of computer science is seeking to keep in touch with its alumni after they graduate.
In an effort to make this happen we have established a platform that will serve the purpose of bridging the
communication gap among its alumni and more especially the interspace between the department and its alumni.

Why

Relationships formed at university are often one of the most important for a person that is ready to go to
the industry. Connections are hard to maintain due to a lack of a centralized platform that connects everybody.
Alumni networks is a platform that aims to bridge communication gaps and help everyone to stay in formed and in touch.
